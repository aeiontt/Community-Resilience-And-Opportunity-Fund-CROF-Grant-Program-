Who We Are

Community Resilience and Opportunity Fund (CROF) is a mission-driven organization focused on empowering individuals and communities through strategic funding opportunities and development initiatives.

We believe that access to resources can transform lives, strengthen communities, and create lasting economic and social impact.

Our programs are designed to help qualified individuals and organizations pursue educational, business, and community advancement opportunities.
